🚨 Common Warning Signs

💨

Gas Smell

Immediately turn off gas supply and call us - never ignore gas odors

🔥

Yellow Flames

Flames should be blue - yellow flames indicate incomplete combustion

🔊

Unusual Noises

Hissing, popping, or unusual sounds require immediate attention

⚡

Ignition Issues

Difficulty lighting or frequent extinguishing needs professional repair
